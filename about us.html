About Us


Young Protons is a mission to help young people acquire practical skills that they can use in the real world. We are committed to offering free opportunities for learning, sharing knowledge, and connecting with others who have expertise in various areas.
Our goal is to bring together curious minds and create meaningful connections between individuals.

Tech

The Tech Club is a community of students who are passionate about technology and innovation. We organize workshops, hackathons, guest lectures and networking events to help our members learn new skills, explore their interests and connect with like-minded people. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, you are welcome to join us and share your ideas, projects and enthusiasm.

Finance

The finance club is a group of students who are interested in learning about and discussing various topics related to finance, such as investing, budgeting, saving, and entrepreneurship. The club meets regularly to share ideas, resources, and experiences, and to network with other like-minded peers and professionals. The club also organizes workshops, guest lectures, and field trips to enhance the members' knowledge and skills in finance. The club welcomes students from all majors and backgrounds who want to explore the world of finance and improve their financial literacy.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship club is a community of students who are passionate about creating and growing their own businesses. The club provides opportunities for networking, mentoring, learning, and funding for aspiring entrepreneurs. Whether you have an idea, a prototype, or a startup, you can join the club and benefit from the resources and support it offers.

Art and Design

The Art and Design club is a creative community for students who are interested in exploring various forms of art and design. The club organizes workshops, exhibitions, and competitions to showcase and improve the skills of its members. The club welcomes anyone who has a passion for art and design, regardless of their level of experience or background.

Music

Music club is a website where you can discover new songs, artists and genres every day. You can also create your own playlists, share them with other users and join online events. Music club is the perfect place for music lovers of all kinds.

Research

Research club is a group of students who are interested in conducting original research on various topics. The club meets to discuss research ideas, methods, and findings. The club also organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences to share their work with the wider community. Research club welcomes anyone who is curious and passionate about learning new things.
